Milliella was born as a babie in Sussex. He is a multi media artist although works primarly with gouache and acrylic mediums.

He draws inspiration from the colour of emotions, the inner workings of the human body and impressionism. With links to Ai, human creativity and horror. Working mainly on wooden boards and sketchbooks,he's interested in bassically anything and everything to do with art and physical outlets of expression.
